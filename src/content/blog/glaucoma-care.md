Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often due to abnormally high pressure in your eye. It is one of the leading causes of blindness for people over the age of 60.

## Why is it dangerous?
The most common form of glaucoma has no warning signs. The effect is so gradual that you may not notice a change in vision until the condition is at an advanced stage. Because vision loss due to glaucoma can't be recovered, regular eye exams that include measurements of your eye pressure are crucial.

## Management and Treatment
While glaucoma cannot be cured, it can be controlled. Treatment usually involves prescription eye drops, oral medications, laser treatment, or surgery to lower the pressure in your eye. Early diagnosis at Crispr Eye Care is the key to preserving your vision.
