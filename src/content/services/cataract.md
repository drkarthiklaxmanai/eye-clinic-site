A cataract is the clouding of the eye's natural lens, which lies behind the iris and the pupil. It is a normal part of aging, but it can lead to a progressive decrease in vision over time.

At Crispr Eye Care, cataract surgery uses micro-incision phacoemulsification -- a stitchless, painless procedure performed under topical anesthesia, with most patients noticing a dramatic improvement in vision within 24 hours. Once the cataract is removed, Dr. Rajeswari implants a premium intraocular lens (IOL) tailored to your lifestyle and visual needs.
