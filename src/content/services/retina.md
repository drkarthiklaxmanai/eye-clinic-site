Retinal conditions are a leading cause of preventable vision loss, but in most cases that loss can be avoided entirely with timely detection and treatment. Dr. Rajeswari's Medical Retina Fellowship training means evaluation and treatment decisions are based on current evidence, not routine escalation.

Damage to the retina from advanced disease is frequently irreversible. Whether you've been told to screen because of diabetes or age, or you're noticing changes in your vision right now, the right time to get evaluated is before symptoms become severe -- not after.
